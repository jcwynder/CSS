Demonstrating the Box Model

The CSS box model is a box that wraps around every HTML element. It consists of a margin, a boarder, a padding, and the actual content.

This text is the content of the box. We have added a 50px padding, 20px margin and a 15px red border.

Descrpiton of Layouts:

  • Margin: the space around the outside of the border.
  • Boarder: the solid, red line that is just outside the padding.
  • Padding: the space around the content. In the example above, it is the space around the paragraph text.
